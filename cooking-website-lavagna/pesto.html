    Pesto


    Ingredients

    1/2 cloves of garlic

    Basil

    Extra virgin olive oil

    Parmigiano Reggiano

    Pine nuts


    Preparation

    Put all ingredients in a mortar and mix them together (don't crush them like you were using an hammer). That's it.


    If you don't have a mortar, you can use a blender. Use the lowest speed (it's important for the pesto ingredients to lose all the essential oils).
